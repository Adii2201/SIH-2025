3D Medical Visualization

The Problem We Solve

Ineffective Learning

65% of medical students are kinesthetic learners. Traditional 2D methods & costly cadavers fail them.

Surgical Risk

30% of unexpected complications stem from poor visualization of patient-specific anatomy from 2D scans.

Patient Anxiety

72% of patients experience high pre-op anxiety due to a lack of procedural understanding.

Our Solution

AnatomX is a Unity-based software platform which offers fully interactive, photorealistic 3D models. It serves two core markets:

  • For Education: Immersive learning module for anatomy exploration, dissection, and quizzes.
  • For Surgery: Pre-op planning simulator and visual communication tool for doctors and patients.

AnatomX Interface Preview

Key Features

Patient-Specific Focus

Moves beyond generic models to user-friendly, personalized model generation.

Procedural Simulation

Integrates physics-based cutting, clamping, and suturing—not just passive visualization.

High Accessibility

Cost-effective solution with a target student license < ₹250/year.
